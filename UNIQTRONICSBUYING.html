What can i do with Uniqtronics Buying ?

Price & Inventory Search

UNIQTRONICS BUYING offers a single place to view up-to-date pricing and inventory from the world's largest distributors.

Part Alerts

You can now set alerts for inventory and pricing changes for a specific manufacturer part number to receive email notifications based on your settings. Part Alerts are available from the Part Intelligence page.

Part Intelligence

Use the Part Intelligence tab to find deep insights into part details, alternates, purchasing and availability trends. You can also valuable resources like models, footprints, and symbols and reference designs.

Parametric Search

Use the Parametric Search functionality to narrow down your part options by part category and our expansive part attribute filtering.

Part Comparison

With our Part Comparison feature, members of the procurement and engineering teams can immediately see a detailed breakdown of the similarities and differences between any two parts. Try this tool from the Part Intelligence Page or the Parametric Search results.
